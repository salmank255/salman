Salman Khan

Research Fellow, Visual Artificial Intelligence Laboratory, Oxford Brookes University, Oxford, UK

E-138, Wheatley Campus, Oxford Brookes, University

Oxford (OX33 1HX), United Kingdom

I am a Research Fellow at the Visual Artificial Intelligence Laboratory, Oxford Brookes University. I have completed my PhD in 2023 under the supervision of Prof. Fabio Cuzzolin at the same institution. Prior to this, I completed my Master’s in 2020 at Sejong University, South Korea, supervised by Prof. Sung Wook Baik and Dr. Khan Muhammad. My research focuses on action and activity recognition/detection, epistemic uncertainty, continual learning, surgical robotics, and fire & smoke detection/segmentation. I am deeply committed to pushing the boundaries of AI’s capabilities and collaborating with fellow researchers to address complex challenges in the field.
